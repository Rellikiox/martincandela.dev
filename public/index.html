About Me

I'm an engineer through and through, I love cracking problems and finding efficient solutions, it's what I do best. It began during primary school, where I would sneak away to the computer lab between classes, and it became real when I got a Master's in Computer Science. My primary focus has been on backend technologies, but I also have experience in frontend development through personal projects, and I'm always eager to learn new things.

I have spent the last 8 years working on Google's Digital Garage, an e-learning platform with over 10M users. From its conception and the development of the first prototype, to its launch on a single market, its expansion to over 50 markets, and finally shutting it down after migrating its content and users to a new home in the Google ecosystem. During my time working on this project I went from a developer, to the principal developer, to the tech lead of the whole program. Now that this project is being sunset I'm looking for a new project that offers the opportunity to further develop and utilize my skills, while collaborating with talented professionals.

Throughout my carrer I've always made an effort to make systems as efficient as possible. I can't stand it when websites make me wait, so I always make it a point to optimize every project I work on from its inception. That means they run faster, are cheaper to operate, and the users are happier. I also love is tackling technical debt. I enjoy diving into a system, figuring out what it really needs to do, and getting rid of anything that doesn't contribute to that goal.

When I'm not working or juggling parent duties I like to wind down cooking, creating small videogames, playing them, and reading.
