About Me

I'm a developer by trade, it's what I do best. My primary focus has been on backend technologies, but I have also gained experience in frontend development through personal projects, and I'm always eager to learn new things.

I have spent the last 8 years working on Google's Digital Garage, an e-learning platform with over 10M users. From its conception and the development of the first prototype, to its launch on a single market, its expansion to over 50 markets, and finally shutting it down after migrating its content and users to a new home in the Google ecosystem. Now that this project is being sunset I'm looking for a new project that offers the opportunity to further develop and utilize my skills, while collaborating with talented professionals.

I can't stand it when websites make me wait, so I always make it a point to optimize the sites I work on to be as efficient as possible. That means they run faster, are cheaper to operate, and the users are happier. Another thing I love is tackling technical debt. I enjoy diving into a system, figuring out what it really needs to do, and getting rid of anything that doesn't contribute to that goal.

When I'm not working or juggling parent duties I like to wind down cooking, creating small videogames (and playing them) and reading.
